About Us

About Abu Usman Hospital and Health Services

Trusted healthcare built on compassion, innovation, and excellence.

Abu Usman Hospital and Health Services is a trusted healthcare provider based in Peshawar, Pakistan, offering comprehensive medical facilities, experienced consultants, and advanced healthcare solutions. Along with hospital-based treatment, we also provide healthcare services at patients' homes, making quality healthcare more accessible.

Our hospital is equipped with modern operation theatres, advanced diagnostic equipment, highly qualified doctors, experienced nursing staff, and compassionate healthcare professionals dedicated to delivering safe, efficient, and patient-centered care.

Our mission is to provide healthcare built on trust, compassion, innovation, affordability, and excellence while ensuring every patient receives the highest standard of treatment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver safe, compassionate, and high-quality healthcare by combining advanced medical technology with experienced healthcare professionals. We strive to improve lives through accessible, affordable, and patient-focused healthcare while maintaining the highest standards of clinical excellence.

Our Vision

To become one of Pakistan's most trusted healthcare providers by delivering world-class medical services, embracing innovation, supporting our consultants, and continuously improving patient outcomes while maintaining integrity and compassion.
